Rasmea

Leader: King Ferrin Hawkash, human. 44 years old.
Government: Limited heriditary autocracy. Feudal style lords have their own lands and say in governance. A majority of lord can legally overpower the monarch in domestic policy, but foreign policy is his exclusive domain. The heir apparent, Oaken Hawkash, 22, is a respected fighter in tournaments, a notable strategist and cunning political player. Doven Hawkash is the second prince, 19, who is installed in the Merchant's guild.
Standing: Aside from the King's personal retinue and army, he has monarchical levies from his vassals. At full fighting strength, the Rasmean army is second only to the army of Valia.

Has no access to open water, so operates a small fleet of rivergoing vessels from a "naval base" inside GreyWallow. The wharfs are in an open area inside the city, which is a hub of trade. There is a more serious dock town in northern Voration that the canal boats serve

Arnia

DeFacto Leader: Senator Dorif Longbrew, Dwarf
Government: Senatorial republic. One representative from each district is sent to sit at the High Table in Heartvale. Legally they are of equal power, but the representative from Kham Ulihm is highly respected and holds sway over the rest of the senate.
Standing: A powerful defensive army with very little projection. The standing army is multiracial but is majority dwarven, and sit in a series of low, defensible forts along the narrow easy passages through the forests.

Major cities/towns

City name Population Known for
Heartvale 65,000 Utilizes the lake and surrounding wildlands. Small shipyard. Fairly quiet.
Kham Ulihm 11,000 The most productive hematite mine in the known world. The hematite is also found in a massive solid vein of marble, so many marble products come from here.

Edruadal

Leader: Llamiryl Vennan
A verdant leaf crown with red flowers splitting a field of green over black. It symbolizes the blood spilled in splitting the forest from the darkness after the Cataclysm.
Government: A willing dictatorship. There is no real national government or standing army. Various loose enclaves have their own militias that are willing to gather for times of need. Representatives can petition the "dictator" for assistance but it is rare. Enclaves are traditionally to maintain self-reliance. Much of the economy is based on trade goods, service, hunting and gathering, or community agriculture.
Standing: Erdruadal is respected in general, but their political power is considered nil due to their policy of war-avoidance.

Valia

Leader: King Valgian
Government: Total monarchy.
Standing: A rising economic powerhouse with a large, regimented standing army. 10 years of service grants permanent minor title and land grant. 20 years of service grants permanent minor title and a major land grant. This is leading to a burgeoning fragmentary feudal system with thousands of tiny lords in their tiny fiefdoms. A small army of tax investigators collects tax on all taxes gathered by the minor lords. Some settlers hate when the lord comes to town and imposes his taxes, because it brings a sudden end to their settler lifestyle.
